Moisture Management

Why Encapsulate Your Foundation?

In North Carolina, a vented crawlspace is a liability. We turn that damp, dark area into a clean, dry environment that protects your home's structural integrity.

  • Healthier Air Quality

    50% of the air you breathe in your living room comes from your crawlspace. We stop mold spores and allergens before they enter your home.

  • Energy Efficiency

    A sealed crawlspace stabilizes your home's temperature, reducing your summer cooling and winter heating bills by up to 15%.

  • Structural Protection

    High humidity leads to wood rot and sagging floors. Our heavy-duty 20-mil barriers keep your floor joists dry and strong for life.

The Transformation
